Biography

Ron Christian A. Aperocho holds a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering from the University of Santo Tomas, Sampaloc, Manila,Philippines in 2017 and passed the Electrical Engineering Board Exam by PRC in the same year. In March 2018, he worked in Therma Marine Inc.-AboitizPower, Nasipit, Agusan del Norte as an electrical engineer. He was responsible in conducting preventive and corrective maintenance on all Low voltage and High voltage equipment,maintenance of back-up batteries, auxiliary generator as well as monitoring the 2 x 50 MW Diesel Generators. From January 2019-October 2019, after a promotion, his next role was as an Electrical and Instrumentation Engineer and from October 2019-August 2020, he was assigned to the operations department as a Control Room Engineer. In August 2020 he joined South Luzon Thermal Energy Corporation as a Condition Based Monitoring Engineer in which he is responsible for the monitoring of all rotating equipment using condition-based tools such as vibration monitoring, oil analysis and ultrasonic testing. He has a certified ISO 18436-2 Category II Vibration Analyst.
